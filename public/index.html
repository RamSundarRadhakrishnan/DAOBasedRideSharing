The Core Protocol

PROFIT, REDISTRIBUTED.

  • Zero Platform Hegemony: By removing central authority, we prevent "shifting goalposts" and ensure the community sets the standards.
  • Fiat-Settled, DAO-Governed: We use fiat for daily payments to keep things simple, while the Colony DAO manages the underlying trust.
  • Reputation as Equity: Your "Reputation" tokens represent your influence. They are earned through safe behavior, cannot be traded, and decay over time to ensure only active, high-quality contributors lead the grid.

The Safety Manifesto

BUILT ON TRUST, VERIFIED BY CODE.

  • Democratic Justice: Disputes aren't handled by black-box AI. They are resolved by high-reputation human arbitrators within the DAO.
  • Real-Time Verification: Every mile is tracked via GPS and hardware oracles to ensure the "source of truth" is never in question.
  • Safety First: Integrated SOS features and identity verification (KYC) ensure every Node in the Lattice is a verified member of the community.
